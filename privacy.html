Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Sparkle Joker, developed by Pippin Team, stores local application information and how certain technical information may be processed through third-party advertising and attribution services.

1. Application Operator

Sparkle Joker is operated by Pippin Team. Questions about this Privacy Policy may be sent to zohazibuq788@gmail.com.

2. No User Accounts or Profiles

Sparkle Joker does not require registration, login, account creation, or profile creation. The application does not provide an account deletion feature because no user accounts or profiles are created or maintained by the application.

3. Local Game Progress and Preferences

Sparkle Joker may store game progress, achievements, selected preferences, and related in-app settings locally on your device.

This local information is not a user account or profile. Sparkle Joker does not provide registration, login, account creation, or profile creation.

You may remove locally stored app information by clearing the application's locally stored data where your device provides that option, or by uninstalling the application, subject to your device platform behavior.

4. Information That May Be Collected Automatically

When you use Sparkle Joker, certain technical and usage-related information may be collected automatically through third-party software development kits integrated into the application, including Google AdMob and Tenjin.

This information may include:

  • device identifiers, including advertising identifiers where available;
  • device type, operating system version, language, and general technical information;
  • IP address and approximate location derived from technical network information;
  • application interactions, ad views, ad clicks, and session-related events;
  • installation, attribution, and campaign performance information;
  • diagnostic or performance information needed to operate and measure services.

5. Advertising Through Google AdMob

Sparkle Joker uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. In connection with ad delivery, ad measurement, fraud prevention, and related advertising functionality, Google may collect or process information such as device identifiers, advertising identifiers, IP address, app interactions, ad interactions, and diagnostic information.

Depending on applicable requirements, device settings, region, and consent choices, advertisements may be personalized or non-personalized.

Additional information about Google's privacy practices is available in the Google Privacy Policy.

6. Attribution and Measurement Through Tenjin

Sparkle Joker uses Tenjin for mobile attribution, installation measurement, and advertising campaign performance analysis. Tenjin may process technical, device, installation, interaction, and attribution-related information in order to provide these services.

Additional information about Tenjin's privacy practices is available in the Tenjin Privacy Policy.

7. How Information Is Used

Information processed in connection with the application may be used to:

  • store local game progress and preferences on your device;
  • display and measure advertisements;
  • understand app installations and advertising campaign performance;
  • support attribution and analytics functionality;
  • detect fraud, abuse, or invalid advertising activity;
  • maintain and improve the performance and reliability of the application and related services.

8. Sharing and Sale of Data

Pippin Team does not sell user personal data. Local game progress, achievements, selected preferences, and in-app settings are stored on your device as described above.

Separate technical and usage-related information may be transmitted to and processed by third-party service providers integrated into the application, including Google AdMob and Tenjin, for advertising, attribution, measurement, analytics, security, and service operation purposes described in this Policy.

These providers process information in accordance with their own terms and privacy policies.

9. Data Storage and Retention

Local game progress, achievements, selected preferences, and application settings are stored locally on your device and remain available until removed through device-supported application data controls or by uninstalling the application, subject to platform behavior.

Pippin Team does not operate a user account database for Sparkle Joker. Information collected through Google AdMob and Tenjin may be stored and retained by those service providers in accordance with their respective privacy policies, contractual terms, configurations, and applicable law.

10. User Choices and Device Controls

You may remove local app content, such as game progress and preferences, using available device controls for clearing app data or by uninstalling the application, subject to the behavior of your device platform.

Depending on your device and region, you may also be able to control advertising identifiers, ad personalization, tracking permissions, or consent choices through your device settings or consent interfaces presented in the app.

11. Children's Privacy

Sparkle Joker does not provide account registration, profile creation, or online profile submission. Advertising and measurement services used in the application may be subject to additional configuration and legal requirements depending on the intended audience and distribution settings of the application.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated when the application, integrated services, legal requirements, or data processing practices change. Any updated version will be published on this page with a revised effective date.

13. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy or the privacy practices of Sparkle Joker, contact: zohazibuq788@gmail.com.
