Privacy & Transparency

Privacy Policy

This page explains the privacy approach used for Sparkle Joker. The goal is to keep the policy simple, direct, and easy to understand for both users and reviewers.

No Data Collection

Sparkle Joker does not collect personal data, non-personal data, usage profiles, account data, or device analytics from users.

No Data Storage

The application does not store user data on external servers, developer systems, or cloud databases connected to this product.

No Data Sharing

No user information is sold, transferred, shared, or disclosed to third parties because the app does not gather such information in the first place.

No Tracking Tools

The app is presented without user tracking mechanisms, advertising SDKs, or analytics systems intended to monitor individual behavior.

Simple App Experience

Sparkle Joker is designed to provide a straightforward user experience without requiring account registration or personal profile submission.
